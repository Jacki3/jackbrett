VStore

Collaboration with VitaeVR, a subsidiary company of Outpost VFX, Sony, Epic Games and the KCL psychology department. Developed novel functions and helped port VStore from PC (Vive) to PSVR. VStore is a novel functional cognition VR shopping task created in UE4 (C++ & visual scripting) with the intent to increase the ecological validity of cognitive assessments, specifically Dementia and Alzheimers.
